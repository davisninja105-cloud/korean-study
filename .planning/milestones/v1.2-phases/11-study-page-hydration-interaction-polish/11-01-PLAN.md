<objective>
Establish the shared foundation for the study-page RSC conversion (RSC-02): extract the inline DTO types from `app/cards/page.tsx` into a new `lib/dto.ts`, migrate the existing cards-page consumers to import from there, and extract the entire due-card selection pipeline from `app/api/cards/due/route.ts` into a server-only `lib/study-cards.ts` that returns serialized `CardDTO[]`.

Purpose: Per D-02 and D-05, both the study RSC page and the existing `/api/cards/due` route must call one shared, DRY pipeline, and both RSC pages must share one DTO type source. This plan creates those two shared modules without yet wiring the study page (that is Plan 02). It is the prerequisite for Plan 02.

Output: `lib/dto.ts` (shared DTO types), `lib/study-cards.ts` (shared server-only pipeline), and migrated imports in `app/cards/page.tsx` + `components/CardsClient.tsx`.
</objective>
