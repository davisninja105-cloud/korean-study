<objective>
Extract the sentence-selection algorithm and its `hashStr` dependency out of `components/StudySession.tsx` into a new pure, tested module `lib/sentence-selection.ts`, then wrap the call site in `useMemo`. This is the first, locked step of the Phase 15 sequencing note (REFACTOR-02 + PERF-03 land together, BEFORE the mode-component split in Plan 02) so the selection logic is restructured exactly once.

Purpose: Make sentence selection independently testable (REFACTOR-02) and stop it recomputing on every unrelated re-render — typing in the fill-blank input, MC selection, `exampleOffset` cycling, and `cardHeight` measurement currently all re-run the full `chosenIdx` IIFE (PERF-03).

Output: `lib/sentence-selection.ts` (exports `SelectableSentence`, `hashStr`, `selectSentence`), `tests/sentence-selection.test.ts`, and a slimmed `components/StudySession.tsx` whose selection is a memoized call to `selectSentence()` with a single shared `hashStr`.
</objective>
