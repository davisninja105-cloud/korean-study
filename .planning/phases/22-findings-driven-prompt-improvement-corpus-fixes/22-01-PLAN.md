<objective>
Make `sentenceMatch()` word-boundary-aware for single-character targetForms (D-01, D-02, D-05): an isolated single-char token (non-Hangul on both sides — space, punctuation, or string edge) becomes blank-safe; an embedded single-char (adjacent to Hangul) stays unsafe. This is the root-cause fix that (a) makes the audit-flagged card 다 (`cmqlm1w0u014k0gsa6eydclfd`) blank-safe with NO database change (D-03), (b) stops Phase 20's code-enforced blank-safety from silently dropping real single-syllable vocabulary at extraction time, and (c) is the fix-in-place resolution of the audit's one zero-safe finding (FIX-01 — corrected by rule change, not by DB mutation).

Purpose: unblocks every downstream Phase 22 deliverable — the prompt-eval baseline (plan 02) and the post-fix audit re-run (plan 03) both classify blank-safety through this predicate, so it must land first and be identical for all before/after comparisons.
Output: updated `lib/sentence-match.ts` predicate + docstring; updated/expanded `tests/sentence-match.test.ts`.
</objective>
