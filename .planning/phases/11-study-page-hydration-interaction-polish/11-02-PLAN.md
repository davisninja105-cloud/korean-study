<objective>
Convert the study page to the RSC + client-shell pattern so it lands on mode-select instantly on first load (RSC-02), and simplify `/api/cards/due` to delegate to the shared `getStudyCards` pipeline created in Plan 01.

Purpose: The current `app/study/page.tsx` is a `'use client'` component that mounts in `phase='loading'` and runs two serial `useEffect` fetches (`/api/lessons` then `/api/cards/due`) before mode-select appears ‚Äî that is the blank loading flash RSC-02 eliminates. Per D-01/D-04 the cards + lessons are fetched server-side at render time and handed to a new `StudyClient` shell as `initialCards`/`initialLessons`; the shell starts in `'select-mode'`. Per D-06 the lesson-filter re-fetch stays on mode-select with a spinner instead of dropping to a blank loading screen.

Output: simplified `app/api/cards/due/route.ts`, async RSC `app/study/page.tsx`, and new `components/StudyClient.tsx` holding all study interactivity.
</objective>
