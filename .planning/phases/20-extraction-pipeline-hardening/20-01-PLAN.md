Output: Rewritten `parseExtractionResponse` + `findLastTopLevelCardBoundary` (depth-2 rule), new exported `normalizeExtractedCards(rawCards, deckSet)`, migrated + extended `tests/extract-cards.test.ts`.
</objective>
